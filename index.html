About Me


I’m Oliver. I’m 22 years old and from Oldham. That’s it, I’ve never moved anywhere else!

There are many things I like, but the one I’m most passionate about are games. Just to name a few of my favourite games:

  • Fire Emblem
  • Apex Legends
  • Bayonetta
  • Borderlands
  • Animal Crossing
  • Super Smash Bros
  • Pokemon
  • Dragon Quest

and many more!

Games have always been a big part of life. My dream since I was a child was to be a game designer, which would explain why I studied Games design in college and obtained a Bachelors degree (with Honours) in games art. Although it doesn't officially say it, I like to specialise in Asset and Background creation.

I could now make 3D and 2D images and models, but there's so much more to making a game! One of these parts is coding. This was a topic I attempted to try my hand in previously, but never managed to keep it going. Thankfully, I enrolled onto Code Nation, where I realise I could finally learn to code and thus be able to make my own game and have a new skill to add to my repertoire.

My experience with coding is very positive. Prior to the course, looking at code was like looking at a foreign language. Now, I am able to piece together what code does what and how to build it up myself using this knowledge. In time, I can only get better and better.
