Outcome 1.4

Describe the risks that might impact digital projects

While Technology and IT is a vast improvement to business and projects, it does not come without it’s fair share of risks. For example, using an older laptop or desktop is more likely to crash and encounter problems with than a newer model. Because of this, it is more likely to lose vital information or parts of a project [1]. Another problem we could encounter is not adjusting for it after the project is finished and/or gone live. Many problems relating to code might not appear until the final part is pushed and while it might be easy to fix, leaving this problem late into the design cycle may cause problems for bigger companies [3]. Not being clear on what needs doing first within a project or pushing away higher priority tasks can also take it’s toll on the projects workflow or even the deadline [2].
